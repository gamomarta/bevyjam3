Tranquil General

OUTBREAK!!!
People with a mild fungal infection are running towards the hospital.
If they get there, they will infect people, and if everyone is sick, they will sneeze for a few days, and that's a losing condition if I ever saw one.
Place well-trained doctors armed with experimental pills to kill gently treat the infected. But watch out, every medicine has its side effects.
How to play: Click around.
